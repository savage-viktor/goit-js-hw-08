how it’s made?

We follow the world trends in the production of natural ice cream so that you can enjoy delicious ice cream made with the most honest ingredients.

Our ice cream contains six natural ingredients. We use perfect milk from our organic farms and Columbian cane sugar. Other ingredients include cream, butter, fruits, and berries. We also produce frozen juice.

We use only eco-friendly packaging.

Every product is randomly tested. We also strictly monitor the ingredients we purchase from outside suppliers.
